Who We Are

A Youth-Led Movement for Change

Youth Leaders Organization (YOLO), formerly known as Barog Kabataan, is a community-based youth organization founded on September 24, 2024 in El Salvador City, Misamis Oriental by John Loyd Tulabing.

Our programs and operations extend throughout the entire Province of Misamis Oriental, reaching young people aged 15 to 30 who are passionate about leadership, advocacy, and community service.

We are registered under the Youth Organization Registration Program (YORP) of the National Youth Commission.


Community-Based YO YORP Registered El Salvador City Misamis Oriental

🎯 Our Vision

A generation of empowered, principled, and socially responsible youth leaders who are active contributors to the development of their communities and the nation.

🚀 Our Mission

Cultivating youth leadership and civic engagement, promoting human rights, advancing mental health awareness, and improving access to quality education through community-based programs and partnerships.

Join YOLO

We're looking for passionate young leaders aged 15–30 from El Salvador City or anywhere in Misamis Oriental who are ready to serve, lead, and grow.

Eligibility Requirements

  • Must be 15–30 years old
  • Resident of El Salvador City or any municipality in Misamis Oriental
  • Not a member of any political youth organization (e.g., United Youth, Paglaum Youth)
  • Willing to commit to YOLO's vision, mission, and values
  • Preference given to active student leaders or community youth leaders
